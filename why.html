Why I Built FounderOS

I built FounderOS after seeing a founder friend buried in physical mail and paperwork. There were tools for pitch decks, CRMs, and chat — but nothing to handle the boring ops that pile up.

This site is a quiet experiment in making the back office invisible. Just done — without checklists, dashboards, or chaos.
